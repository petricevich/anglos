About Us

Invested a great deal of effort in a project and wish to see it cross borders? Looking to move your business forward by harnessing foreign language sources or need a document translated? We're Anglos, a translation and interpretation agency offering quality services – with dedication, an individual approach and on time. We offer the following services: translation, interpretation (simultaneous and consecutive), court interpreter services and conference equipment rentals.

TRANSLATIONS

We take a thorough approach to every text and always strive to achieve more. This has won us relevant experience in translating texts in many domains: legal (regulations, directives and other legislative acts of the EU, laws and regulations, court documentation, contracts, legal opinions etc.), finance and accounting (bank, factoring company and insurance provider documentation, financial reports, accounting standards etc.), environmental protection and energy (environmental impact studies, oil and gas studies) and other (business correspondence, personal ID, conference, seminar and workshop materials, and technical specifications). Since we approach every text as a separate project deserving full attention, our pricing is based on the text itself, i.e. its complexity and quantity and the required deadline.

COURT INTERPRETER SERVICES

We are authorised to provide court interpreter services, which include certified translations of legal documents and other documentation for official purposes in Croatia and abroad (birth and marriage certificates, work and education permits, diplomas, court documentation, tender documentation, powers of attorney, excerpts from court registers and the like) and interpretation before a court and other legal bodies in the Republic of Croatia.

SIMULTANEOUS AND CONSECUTIVE INTERPRETATION

Organising a workshop or conference with foreign speakers, or wish to take part in an event of this kind? We will see to it that your ideas come across completely and clearly and that communication is fluid. Our interpreters are professional, reliable and flexible. They make thorough preparations for every project and know how to express ideas from one language into another in the method that best suits you (simultaneous or consecutive). As with translation services, interpretation is personalised and based on a number of factors so feel free to request a quote and pricing.

EQUIPMENT RENTALS

Need simultaneous interpretation equipment for an event you are organising? We can help with that too. We offer rentals of the necessary equipment for the best interpretation: interpreter booths, receivers with headphones and a sound system, as well as technicians to install and monitor the equipment.
